# Market Aspirations Alignment (Step 6)

## Strategic outcomes
- Faster checkout and better customer experience.
- Lower shrinkage risk through inventory-cash consistency.
- Better operational control for multi-cashier environments.

## Suggested KPIs
- POS checkout completion rate.
- Average checkout time per sale.
- Cash reconciliation mismatch rate.
- Stock drift incidents linked to POS operations.

## AI-era readiness
- Structured POS events to enable anomaly detection.
- Deterministic flow states for automated incident triage.
