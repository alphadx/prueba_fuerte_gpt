# Market Aspirations Alignment (Step 1)

## Strategic outcomes
- Faster time-to-market by reducing requirement churn before implementation.
- Better product-market fit through measurable outcome-oriented scope.
- Stronger stakeholder trust with traceable acceptance and evidence.

## Suggested KPIs
- Requirement change rate after development start.
- % of stories accepted in first QA pass.
- Lead time from scope approval to first executable increment.
- # of critical ambiguities detected after sprint start.

## AI-era readiness
- Keep stories explicit and structured to enable AI-assisted test generation.
- Use machine-parsable acceptance criteria to automate validation.
